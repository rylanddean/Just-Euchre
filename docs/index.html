Rules

How to Play

01

You and your partner vs. two bots. Work together or go it alone.

02

One suit is trump each round. The Jack of that suit — the Right Bower — is the most powerful card in the game.

03

Win tricks to score points. First team to 10 points wins the game.

04

24-card deck — 9s through Aces, four suits. Fast games, deep strategy.
