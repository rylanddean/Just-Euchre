Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 27, 2026

The short version: Just Euchre collects no personal data, creates no accounts, and sends nothing off your device. Your game is yours.

Overview

Just Euchre ("the App") is a daily card game for iOS. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy describes what data the App collects, how it is used, and your rights as a user.

Data We Collect

Just Euchre does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers. The App has no user accounts, no login, and no backend.

All game data — including your streak, game history, and settings — is stored locally on your device using Apple's standard on-device storage (UserDefaults / app sandbox). This data never leaves your device.

Push Notifications

The App may request permission to send you daily push notifications as reminders to play your game of the day. These notifications are scheduled locally on your device using iOS notification APIs. No notification content or metadata is transmitted to any server.

You can disable notifications at any time in Settings → Notifications → Just Euchre.

Analytics & Tracking

Just Euchre contains no third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking libraries. We do not use:

  • Crash reporting services
  • Advertising networks or identifiers (IDFA)
  • Analytics platforms (e.g. Firebase, Mixpanel, Amplitude)
  • Session recording or heatmap tools

Third-Party Services

The App is distributed via the Apple App Store. Apple may collect certain usage data in accordance with their own privacy policy. Just Euchre itself does not integrate any third-party services or SDKs beyond Apple's own frameworks.

Children's Privacy

Just Euchre does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Because no data is collected by the App, it is safe for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a revised date. Material changes will be noted in the App's release notes.
