Common Questions
Why can I only play one game per day?
That's the whole point. One game a day keeps it fresh and meaningful. Your streak is built on consistency, not marathon sessions. Come back tomorrow for a new deal.
What is the Right Bower?
The Right Bower is the Jack of the trump suit — the most powerful card in the game. The Left Bower is the Jack of the same-color suit, and it's the second most powerful card. For example, if Hearts are trump, the Jack of Hearts is Right Bower and the Jack of Diamonds is Left Bower.
What is Loner mode?
When you call trump, you can choose to go alone — sending your partner out of the round. If you win all 5 tricks by yourself, you score 4 points instead of the normal 2. It's a high-risk, high-reward move.
My streak broke but I played today. What happened?
Streaks track consecutive wins, not consecutive plays. To keep your streak alive you need to win the game — reach 10 points before the opposing team does. If you think there's a bug involved, let me know.
Does the app collect any of my data?
No. Everything stays on your device. No accounts, no analytics, no tracking. See the full Privacy Policy for details.
How do I turn off daily notifications?
Go to your iPhone's Settings → Notifications → Just Euchre and toggle them off. You can also adjust the notification time inside the app's settings.
